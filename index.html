How It Works

1

Start in the Changing Room

The opening scene begins in a realistic changing room where you can walk around using your mouse or touch controls.

2

Listen to Your Relaxation Coach

Click and listen to the calming voice of your relaxation coach (Clinical Hypnotherapist) with interactive relaxation videos.

3

Enter the Pool

Enter the pool at any point (jump in) or via the pool stairs in the shallow end. Experience what it's like to be in a large swimming pool.

4

Practice Swimming Skills

Submerge, move underwater, go to the deep end, float on your back and front. Try different movements and combinations safely.
